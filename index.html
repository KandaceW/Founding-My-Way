Hi and welcome to my core identity and values blog. A rather long Intro about me

First off let me start with the value I hold in the most highest regard is without a doubt loyalty.̉ This word is thrown around and disrespected by many but for me it is everything. It can represent many other important values including integrity and honesty. Two more words I hold dear to me. I feel if you show loyalty to something, someone or even somewhere then this must mean its important to you. Or you have a connection or a history that defines a small or large part of you. If you have to earn loyalty or have your loyalty be earned it must mean something to you and the other party. Why is it such a big deal? To uphold loyalty is not hard, but it is rear and it is something that I have tried my entire life to keep my standards high and not let influences tell me what I feel to be true or false. I don’t give my loyalty away but I let it grow with anybody I meet. Everything and everyone has the ability to gain my loyalty and give in return. I define Loyalty by some key words trustworthy dependable accountable, honest, integrity and unbreakable. In any case if you are loyal you will make the right decision for that moment.


An example that I can think of to display loyalty from my perspective. Im in a room,someone that I do care for maybe gets mentioned perhaps incorrectly or negativly and false. I would stick up for my friend in their absence. I would correct whatever was being said. I would not participate in any conversation about someone unless I was willing to say it to their face and I am not shy to speak the truth to anyone. My loyalty is within myself I will never go behind a friends back. Another example I can think of from my 21st birthday party (I don’t remember much about my 21st) what I do remember is the first few lines out of my Fathers mouth during his speech. He started off with “If there is one thing I could not say enough times over and the one thing that will not faulter is her tenacious and relentless Loyalty she shows to those dear to her. It is unbreakable and with that loyalty if you have her on side she will make one hell of a guard dog“ he was and is right. My outspoken forward and assertive nature in-fact can be to my detriment sometimes although getting in trouble to up hold my values including on another behalf is never in my mind going to be the wrong decision.


Another value and a identity defining factor about me is the importance of family. The word family means more than the people that raised you with the same blood. It’s also no doubt a choice. I believe that those close to us that we love respect and depend on, that we want to show loyalty to and care for from our hearts are the people that matter. They are our Family. I firmly believe you can pick your family. You are born without choice but as you grow and learn and see the good and evil in the world its important to address the ones you love and believe in you and remember them. For one day when you may need someone if they are true they will be there for you. Everything is a choice. What and who you put your time and energy into will shape you and guide you and its all held in your Gut and Heart. As far as I’m concerned blood does not mean family. Its an identifiable quality we all have but we also all have out own minds and our own hearts and souls which far out weigh the red running through our veins. We are lucky if we have a family that we are born into that we can depend on it is unfortunately not always this way for some but as I said YOU CAN CHOOSE and you can establish your own roots and guide your own children and help shape them and create your family with those dear to you all around you!


While on this subject… How about a bit of my family history! Kandace Ivy Willcox Born to Linda Troy Eccelston & John Stuart Willcox in Welington womans hospital 29/10/1984. Second born of 4 siblings. Michael, Toyah Ashleigh and myself. We were bought up in Wellington and I am proud of where I came from. I am 34 I have two children. My beautiful daughter Alexandra 10 years and my gorgeous son Luke 8 next week. Alex is an amazing and honest soul she is wise and she’s also reserved with judgement. Alex is extremely close with her cousins and any newbies added to the mix like blended family for example. My daughter reminds me daily about family values about strength in love and backing each other. My gorgeous girl is an inspiration to me Im more than proud.


My son Luke, he reminds me the small things don’t matter his inner light and diversity shine as he enters a room so does an energy of positivity. He has a natural giving nature and just says yes to things. His acceptance and light heartedness make it easy to gain perspective. The reason Im sharing with you my feelings on my children is to give backing to my own values mentioned above. If I see those qualities in my children I have done something right. I must have a strong influence in their way of thinking. I raised them so far with their Father of course. I believe that strong influence from me and everyone around them including our self appointed family members, their community surrounding us are all an influence and shape us to be who we are!

You remind me so much of myself as a working mum focused on providing the best tasty nutrition, with love & pride in their children thus creating & raising strong, kind, healthy & happy loving children. You've also shown me so much kindness, support when ive been down & non judgemental acceptance. Getting to know me & really knowing me, my morals & values & speaking up for my character in my absence. You take the time to listen & learn about me. There was a dark time for me towards the end of my Salon ownership I was faced with a dilemma that really challenged me and my values. I had a toddler and a baby and I was facing single motherhood, I battled depression and anxiety and felt torn by my stubborn loyalty to my staff and business. I felt scared and immense guilt that I was neglecting my kids and I felt ridiculous pressure to uphold responsibilities and obligations to staff whome depended on me for their livelihoods. I realised pride was playing a big factor and when I spoke with my staff they showed me compassion. Said that they were thankful for me regardless and to do what is right! I sold the business and moved forward in my life and spent my time focused on my kids and I. I remained self employed but on a home basis and have done this for the past 5-6 years. I don’t regret a thing now and I learnt a lot and consider it one of the hardest phases in my life. Its hard to think that for a few years when my kids were little and I owned a business, ran uoto 6 staff, obeyed the law requirments, managed client relationships and a challenging personal relationships and more. I was actually un happy person, both within myself and outwardly to the world. I am an emotional person and this stirs up many memories some painful, there are many feelings and thoughts related to that time.


This is another reason EDA is were I want to be. To ask this of me shows they are wanting the right things from me as I do from them. People are the reason websites exist not technology People are what matter! I want to be apart of the future and shaping people in any way including through Technology! Thanks to the Dev Whanau for even just existing!

Relative - a flexible positioning of an element- where it sits on the page can be moved and place in a position occording to other elements placed on page. in human descriptive terms i would explain it to be for eg. if there was and image on page you would place the element relative to the image by how ever many px for instance. the co-ordanits are bassed on what its workin around... elements are defined to a certain div so the divs essentially if you visualise a div as a box - these are the pieces that are being place on and around each other on page

Absolute - a non flexible positioning of an element. Absolute means that a particularelement is given or assigned a place on the page and becomes cemented there! Its not able to be manipulated by other positions or elemnets it sort of will dictae the other elements to fit around it
